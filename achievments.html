Achievements of Sir Winston Churchill


Led the British through World War II and the 'Blitz' with his inspiring speeches.

He gave money to the development of the tank.

Was knighted by Queen Elizabeth II.

He wrote over 40 books.

Was a very talented Artist.

Won the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1953 and was nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize several times.

Was named an honorary of citizen of the United States of America.

Seved as Prime Minister of Great Britain during World War II and the Cold War.

Was a member of the Most Noble Order of the Garter, the highest order of heroism in the UK
