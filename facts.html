Fun Facts About Winston Churchill

His parents mostly ignored him at young age.

He had poor academic records as a child.

He enjoyed bricklaying and built quite a few walls at his house at Chartwell.

He painted around 500 paintings.

He invented the word "summit" meaning the highest point of something (usually a mountain).

He was known for his smoking and has a cuban cigar named after him.

When he died, he was laid in Westminister Hall, a place where the royal family is buried, for 3 days before being buried inside St. Paul's Cathedral.

Churchill's mom, Jennie Churchill, was American and not British.

Churchill made the term "Iron Curtain" popular.

Churchill was accident prone and was hit by a car in New York.
